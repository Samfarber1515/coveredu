What happens when I turn 26? +
You get removed from your parents' health insurance. It happens at the end of the month you turn 26, regardless of school or job status. You then have a 60-day window to find your own plan.
Is CoveredU actually free? +
Yes! We get paid by insurance carriers when you enroll through us ‚Äî like how Kayak works for flights. You never pay extra. We often find plans cheaper than going direct because we compare every option.
I'm a freelancer. Can I get coverage? +
Absolutely. No employer benefits? You have more options than you think: ACA marketplace plans (many qualify for subsidies), short-term plans, or freelancer-specific options. Our quiz accounts for all of this.
How long does this actually take? +
The quiz: 60 seconds. Reviewing matches: 2-3 minutes. Enrolling: 5-10 minutes. You can go from uninsured to covered in under 15 minutes total.
What if I can't afford insurance? +
You might qualify for subsidies that make plans $0-50/month. Over 60% of marketplace enrollees get financial help. If your income is under ~$60K, there's a good chance you're eligible. Our quiz checks automatically.
