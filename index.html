Quick answers from Covu.

What actually happens when I turn 26? +
Your parents' plan removes you at the end of the month you turn 26. Then you get a 60-day Special Enrollment window to get your own coverage.
Wait, this is actually free? +
Yes. Insurance companies pay us when you enroll through our links, like flight aggregators. You never pay more for using Oopsie.
I'm a freelancer or creator. Can I get coverage? +
Absolutely. Marketplace plans, short-term options, and freelancer-friendly plans are all on the table. We map the cheapest route for your exact situation.
Do you sell my data? +
No. We only use your answers to match plans. Your info is shared with a carrier only when you choose to click through and get a quote.
What if I literally can't afford insurance? +
You might qualify for subsidies that bring plans down to $0-$50/month. If your income is under roughly $60K, there's a good chance you can get financial help.
