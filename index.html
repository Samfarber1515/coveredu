Quick answers.

What happens when I turn 26? +
Most people leave their parents' plan at the end of that month. Then you have 60 days to pick your own plan.
Is this really free? +
Yes. Insurance companies pay us when you sign up through our links. You do not pay extra.
I freelance. Can I still get a plan? +
Yes. We can show plans for freelancers and self-employed people.
Do you sell my data? +
No. We use your answers to match plans. We share your info only when you ask for a quote.
What if I cannot afford insurance? +
You may get help that lowers your monthly cost. We check that for you.
